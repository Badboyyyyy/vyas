  • Message to Students


I have had the extraordinary privilege of guiding the best of the students produced at Kota over the last two decades. But more than the topper, my heart goes out for every child who came to me seeking guidance for his/her academics. In my many years of experience and teaching more than 50000 students, I have seem students understanding a concept in only 5 minutes and being able to solve the questions the next moment, and also those students who have taken upto one complete day to understand the same concept but then getting equal marks in the exams. I understand that every child has a different level of understanding, different grasping power and all- together a different way to understand and connect to a concept. Every student has his/her strength which needs to be identified and strengthened, and weakness which needs to be identified and removed.
We, at Vyas, believe in developing a culture to ensure the sincerity ,learning and growth of students, both inside and outside the classrooms, and hence, to ensure and to contribute to your success. At Vyas, our focus is to work on your strengths and weaknesses along with you so that you are able to conquer your shortcomings and show improved results every time. Not just conceptual learning, we equally work on developing your problem- solving skills and academic learning habits.
The city of Kota has produced toppers. Yes, not every student who comes to Kota gets into IITs, NITs or top medical colleges. But you should always give your best and never give up. Every morning when you wake up, always remember, there is still enough time to study and crack the exam, and to get into a good college. It’s never late to do something good and better in your lives.
For more than two decades, I have offered my heart and soul to this beautiful profession of educating the children and prepare them for their lives, bot just exams. I am proud to offer the same dedicated work at Vyas Edification. Come and learn in the presence of one of the best educators. Come and learn at Vyas-Everything you need to succeed

Regards
Shishir Mittal
Academic Director & Founder – Vyas Edification




Vyas is an Organization dedicated to blending school education seamlessly with top coaching for IIT-JEE,NEET,KVPY,NTSE,DSO,National Olympiads and similar exams
