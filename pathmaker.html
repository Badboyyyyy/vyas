IIT-JEE

Through this four year program, we aim to prepare our students to be successful in JEE-Main & Advanced with best ranks along with excellent results in their board exams. This program also aims for carving success of well-deserving and keen students in IJSO/NTSE/Olympiads.

Description

Entering into Class IX is the awakening time for dreamers of JEE-Main and JEE-Advanced. In four years we intend to develop all the aspects of personality and character of our students. Our program focuses on developing reading, writing and communication skills along with JEE-Main and JEE-Advanced preparation. This four-year program provides the advantage of sufficient time for the preparation of NTSE/IJSO/Olympiads

Courses Plan

The entire course is divided into 162 weeks in which the entire syllabus of IX to XII and JEE-Main and JEE-Advanced will be covered.

Curriculum

The regular classes will be conducted six days a week. In the morning session theory lectures followed by problem-solving techniques, discussion of study materials and test papers will be conducted. In the post-lunch session, students will have a revision session, concept building tests and problem practice of study materials in the supervision of class teachers.

Study Meterial

  • Topic-wise & lecture-wise theory booklets
  • Topic-wise & lecture-wise online DPPs (Daily Practise Problems)
  • Exercise Sheets
  • Board Sheets
  • Memory Maps
  • Revision Sheets
  • Formulae Sheets
  • Chapter Synopsis

Test Pattern

  • Concept Building Tests (Online)
  • Chapter Wise Subjective Tests of Board Syllabus(Offline)
  • Diagnostic Tests(Online)
  • Revision Tests(Online)
  • JEE-Main Pattern Tests(Online)
  • JEE-Advanced Pattern Tests(Online)
  • Board Pattern Tests(Offline)
  • NTSE/IJSO/Olympiads Pattern Tests(Online/Offline)
