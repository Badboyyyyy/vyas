  • Message to Parents


I appreciate and understand how difficult it is to send your child miles away to ensure that he/she clears the prestigious national level competitions and gets into one of the best colleges of India. It clearly shows that you are even willing to part with your child to ensure his/her success. I really appreciate and value all your hard work and sacrifices.

At Vyas, we believe in creating a learning spirit in your child by continuous dedication, determination and hard work. We totally understand that the key to success is not just hard work but a blend of good and healthy academic and non-academic habits. To develop the same, we work on developing your child not just academically but physically and psychologically as well. We work on training him/her for life – developing both IQ (Intelligence Quotient) and EQ (Emotional Quotient). For the overall development of your child, we start the day with yoga and concentration improvement practices which help in improving the mental focus and removing all the diversions and distractions. Nutritious, healthy and hygienic food is offered to all our students. As a mother myself, I always try to ensure that the food provided to all our children is at par with the home food.

We continuously communicate with parents to let them know the current academic standing of their child. We also help parents engage with and support their children’s academic and social-emotional development through various solution-driven techniques.I want you to understand is your child is different and unique in his/her own ways, that we can’t even imagine. He/she is giving his/her best to ensure that one day, all our dreams come true. And we, at Vyas, would work equally hard along with you and him/her to make it possible. I urge you to keep your child motivated always. Motivation and support are all they need from our side. Let us both work collectively to make your child rise and shine and touch the skies.
Regards
Pratiksha Mittal
Managing Director & Founder – Vyas Edification
