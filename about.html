Introduction

Vyas is an organization dedicated to blendinf school education seamlessly with top coaching for IIT-JEE(Main and Advanced),NEET,KVPY,NTSE,IJSO,National Olympiads,as offered by the most trusted faculty members of Kota.It is one of the first organizations in Kota to start Residential-cum-Day Boarding school Integrated Coaching for students from class V to XIII,and XII passed.Our curriculum is not only limited to science education but also brings the same proficiency level to other subjects-English,Hindi,Computers e.t.c

Vision

To evolve,establish and popularize the Bhartiya(Indian)Education system for modern times ,deep rooted in culture and moral values,aimed at the development of students and teacher on physical,mental,emotional,social,economic and spiritual dimensions,with an objective of shaping the best leaders of our country.

Mission

Our mission is to establish a network of leading schools and campuses across the country,which we intend to achieve by combining the benefits of top-notch school education,latest technology and kota's cutting edge to evolve a wholesome,well rounded and world class education for students.
