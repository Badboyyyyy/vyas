IIT-JEE

A good milestone on this path is NSEP/NSEC/NSEA (Physics, Chemistry and Astronomy Olympiad Exams – Stage 01) conducted by the official bodies. Without deviating from the main target of IIT-JEE, success in these exams (usually held in the month of November) is also targeted for the interested students. Intensive training for the students gifted in Maths is also provided for pre-RMO and RMO. Science enthusiasts are specially trained for KVPY exam usually held in January.

Description

The best time to take up the challenge for scoring top ranks in JEE-Main and Advanced along with top ranks in board exams for an aspirant is from class VIII. Our well-articulated program gives our students and edge over all other competitors for JEE-Main & Advanced. The students who take up this course have ample time for the preparation of not only JEE but develop his personality and communication skills. We also aim to provide all round development of our students inducted through this program to mention few fluent speaking English ability, doing extempore and debate, playing indoor and outdoor games and sports. We also provide opportunity to develop some hobbies like singing, dancing, playing instruments, painting etc. How is this Course better than others

Courses Plan

The entire course is divided into 200 weeks in which entire syllabus of VIII to XII and JEE-Main and JEE-Advanced will be covered.

Curriculum

The regular classes will be conducted six days a week. In the morning session theory lectures followed by problem solving techniques, discussion of study materials and test papers will be conducted. In the post lunch session students will have a revision session, concept building tests and problem practice of study materials in the supervision of class teachers.

Study Meterial

  • Daily Practice Problems (DPPs)
  • Modules
  • Board Sheets
  • Memory Maps
  • Revision Sheets
  • Online Test Series
  • Board Practice Tests

Test Pattern

  • Daily Problem Practice
  • Concept Building Tests (Online)
  • Chapter Wise Subjective Tests of Board Syllabus(Offline)
  • Objective Tests(Online)
  • Revision Tests(Online)
  • JEE-Main Pattern Tests(Online)
  • JEE-Advanced Pattern Tests(Online)
  • Board Pattern Tests(Offline)
  • NTSE/IJSO/Olympiads Pattern Tests(Online/Offline)

Special Features

  • Theory lectures: Highly authentic, comprehensive and exhaustive theory lectures having references of nationally and internationally renowned authors and sources.
  • Problem solving techniques: To develop ability for understanding the crux of solving a problem from fundamental level to complex level.
  • Revision & memorization of important formulas/equation & reactions.
  • Concept building test to check the retention of basic fundamental of topics.
  • Exercise session of JEE-Main & Advanced level questions under the supervision of teachers·
  • Discussion of doubts from study materials and test papers·
  • Testing for the preparedness for JEE-Main & Advanced through computer based test·
  • Analysis of test papers and feedbacks of weak topics and additional support·
  • Personalized counselling·
  • Writing and memorizing exercises of board chapters and evaluation of objective/short subjective/long subjective student’s handwritten solutions of board chapters·
  • Board practice test: Chapter wise & model papers for full syllabus once the topics finished·
  • Question reading and attempting techniques in all patterns of tests to minimise errors and increase speed and efficiency
