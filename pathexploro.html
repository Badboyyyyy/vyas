COURSE DESCRIPTION

Through a stimulating classroom environment and high levels of student engagement, the Pinnacle Program empowers students to build on their own learning styles. In doing so, the curriculum actively supports discussion-based learning and hands-on activities, and embeds innovative approaches to learning across all subjects. In addition, the co-curricular activities and service initiatives are integral to student life, nurturing self-aware individuals who are responsible towards themselves and the community. The holistic and experiential nature of learning balances knowledge, understanding and skills, and ensures that students are well-prepared not only for Board Examination but also for National and International competitive exams like NTSE, IJSO, NMTC, SAT/PSAT, KVPY, IPhO, IChO, RMO,ANCQ etc.

Who can apply: For IX to X moving students

Duration: 1 Year
