Ideas Behind Vyas





Dream Cracking




A student comes to Kota with a dream of cracking one of the most prestigious exams such as IIT-JEE or NEET. Tens of thousands of parents also send their children with a hope that one day, their ward would clear these prestigious entrance examinations and make them proud. In fact, more than the struggle which students face at Kota, it is parents who make the actual sacrifice. It is parents who invest not just their money, but their personal life as well. Thousands of mothers stay with their children at Kota, only to ensure that they are getting proper accommodation, food, and care, and to ensure that their child is not lost in the crowd of thousands and lakhs at Kota.
